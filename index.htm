Professional Biography

I'm a web developer and entrepreneur with over 10 years of experience building websites and running businesses. Before founding and working in start-ups, I spent 10 years as a developer creating PC and console games.

I've always had an obsessive attention to detail that I have brought to every product and company I have developed.

Achievements

  • Owner and CTO @WorkFu.
  • Founder of Handy Hippo, a UK e-commerce business specialising in craft supplies. As CEO and CTO I grew the business from zero to a turnover of £750K (acquired 2011).
  • Founder of Synaptic Soup, a gaming middleware business that licensed the 3D rendering technology that I developed.
  • Built a really cool PHP Framework for rapid application development (in fact, it's running this site).

During my career I have largely focused on user experience, efficiency and performance of both software and processes. I also made a lot of games, which was fun.
